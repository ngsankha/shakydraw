About

ShakyDraw generates beautiful hand-drawn flow-diagrams from simple ASCII text art. Use this to generate images for your presentations, blog posts or documentation for free!

This is based on @mraleph's helper tool that converts handwritten ASCII-based diagrams into images.

Have an idea, suggestion or improvement in mind for ShakyDraw? Drop a tweet to @sankha93.
